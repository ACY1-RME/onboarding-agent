🔥 Welcome to the Red Vest Program

At CBRE | Amazon, every task can be done safely — and your safety matters more than any task you'll be asked to do. We don't expect you to be fast yet; we expect you to be safe. Efficiency comes with experience.

Your red vest tells everyone you're new so we can keep an extra eye out for you. You also bring fresh eyes — if something looks off or you wonder why we do it a certain way, speak up and ask. You might have a better way.

You've earned this job. Never take shortcuts or rush to impress us — working safely and asking questions is what truly impresses us. When in doubt, stop and ask.
